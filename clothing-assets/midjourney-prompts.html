Pro Tips for Best Results

Keep Characters Consistent

Generate Super Theo first. Copy his image URL, then add --cref [URL] to every other hero prompt to lock in the same face and style.

Lock the Art Style

After your first image, add --sref [URL] with any generated image URL to keep the same illustration style across ALL characters.

Upscale for Printing

Click U1–U4 on your best result to upscale it to full resolution. Then use Upscale (Subtle) for print quality files.

Multiple Variations

Click V1–V4 on any image to create variations of that specific result. Great for finding the perfect pose.

White Background Tip

If the background isn't perfectly white, use remove.bg (free) to cut out the character instantly for print use.

Character Sheet

Add character sheet, multiple poses, front side back view to get Theo or Flutter from multiple angles — great for illustrators.
