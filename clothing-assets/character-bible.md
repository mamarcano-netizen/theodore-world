# Theodore's World — Official Character Bible

## THEODORE "THEO" (The Boy — Before Powers)

- **Age:** 7 years old
- **Skin:** Warm brown
- **Face:** Round, soft, full of innocence and wonder
- **Eyes:** Big, expressive, dark green — thoughtful, quiet intensity
- **Hair:** Thick, curly dark brown — slightly untamed, soft coils fall across forehead
- **Nose/Cheeks:** Small button nose, round cheeks
- **Smile:** Gentle, slightly shy — lights up his whole face
- **Build:** Small for his age, slim and gentle

**Outfit:**
- Soft, well-worn **lavender T-shirt** with a small **puzzle piece doodled on the chest** (drawn in fabric marker by Theo himself)
- **Dark navy joggers** with elastic waist (sensory-sensitive — no stiff fabrics, no tags)
- Sneakers: **one red, one blue** — always mismatched on purpose, velcro straps (no laces), slightly scuffed
- **Small worn gray backpack** covered in patches/pins:
  - Butterfly pin
  - Gold star
  - Rainbow patch
  - Small **infinity symbol button** (from his dad)

**Inside backpack:**
- Navy blue noise-canceling headphones (worn around neck when not needed)
- Small spiral notebook (draws patterns and shapes)
- Smooth gray worry stone (rubs with thumb when anxious)
- Tiny stuffed butterfly keychain on zipper

**Behavior/Body language:**
- Bounces on toes when excited
- Goes perfectly still when deeply focused
- Tilts head slightly to one side when listening/thinking
- Flaps hands at sides when happy — eyes go wide with pure joy
- When overwhelmed: pulls on headphones, hugs backpack, eyes look down
- When focused: very still, brows furrow slightly, traces patterns with fingertip

---

## THE TRANSFORMATION

The **infinity symbol button** on his backpack begins to glow with warm golden light.
Theo places his hand over it, closes his eyes, and whispers: **"I am enough."**
Golden light bursts outward, swirling around him like a warm wind.
His dark green eyes shift to a powerful, luminous **silver-gray**.
Flutter materializes from the butterfly pin in a shower of gold sparks.

## SUPER THEO (The Hero)

- **Same boy — amplified.** Stands taller, shoulders back, chin up.
- **Skin:** Warm brown with faint golden undertone (lit from within)
- **Hair:** Thick, wavy brown — slightly tousled
- **Eyes:** Striking, luminous **silver-gray** — large, expressive, fierce determination

**Suit:**
- Sleek, form-fitting gradient: **soft pink at shoulders → rich lavender at legs**
- Fabric has subtle shimmer — catches light differently based on mood/energy
- Centered on chest: **bold gold infinity symbol** — slightly raised, metallic, glowing faintly gold

**Cape:**
- **Dark navy** — long and flowing, reaches just past calves
- Inside lining: **faint pattern of tiny gold stars and constellation lines**
- Clasps at collarbone with two small **gold circular pins**

**Boots (MISMATCHED):**
- Left boot: **cherry red**
- Right boot: **bright royal blue**
- Sturdy, rounded-toe, slight platform sole
- Small **gold star on outer ankle** of each boot

**Wrists:** Two slim **gold cuffs** — glow when using powers

**Powers expressed through stims:**
- Hand flaps → **golden sparks fly from fingertips**
- Head tilt → cape shifts, infinity symbol brightens as he locks onto signal
- Finger tracing → maps energy fields, reads emotional landscape

## FLUTTER (The Sidekick)

- **Size:** About the size of a small bird — perches on Theo's shoulder
- **Wings:** Dazzling gradient of **soft gold, warm amber, and iridescent lavender**
  - Shift color depending on angle of light — holographic foil effect
  - Delicate **veining patterns resembling circuit boards / neural pathways**
  - Faint **glowing white outline** on wing edges
- **Body:** Velvety **deep purple**
- **Eyes:** Large, round, **bright turquoise** — incredibly expressive
- **Antennae:** Two long, curling — tipped with tiny **glowing gold orbs** (act as emotional sensors)
- **Trail:** Sparkling **gold dust** follows Flutter wherever it flies (calming properties)

**Mood expressions:**
- Happy: wings shimmer brighter, quick joyful flutters
- Danger: wings pulse deeper amber, antennae point forward like radar

## DR. NORM (Villain — The Conformist)

- **Appearance:** Tall, rigid, unnervingly PERFECT — intentionally bland
- **Skin:** Pale, almost porcelain — no blemishes, no distinguishing features
- **Hair:** Slicked back, perfectly parted, flat lifeless medium brown — not one strand out of place
- **Eyes:** Cold, flat gray — no warmth or spark
- **Glasses:** Thin rectangular silver frames, sitting precisely on straight nose
- **Expression:** Perpetual tight, thin-lipped disapproving smile
- **Jaw:** Sharp and angular
- **Posture:** Stiff, mechanical — every movement calculated

**Outfit:**
- Pristine white lab coat over perfectly pressed **charcoal gray suit** with plain gray tie
- Everything symmetrical and orderly
- Breast pocket pin: **equals sign inside a circle** (symbol of forced sameness)
- Sterile **white latex gloves**
- Belt: small silver tuning-fork-shaped "Normalization Tools"
- Shoes: identical plain black oxfords, polished to mirror shine, perfectly matching

## SENSORY SIREN (Villain — The Overwhelmer)

- **Skin:** Shifting electric pale blue — like static on a TV screen — faint crackling energy lines beneath surface
- **Hair:** Wild, massive chaotic explosion of neon colors: hot pink, electric orange, acid green, blinding yellow — constantly shifting, pulsing, moving like living flames
- **Eyes:** Large, piercing — solid orbs of **blinding white light** — no pupils — emit visible beams when using powers
- **Eyeliner:** Heavy black, sharp angular shapes like warning signs
- **Bodysuit:** Covered in clashing overwhelming patterns — zigzags, spirals, checkerboards, strobe stripes in neon — everything vibrates and moves
- **Collar:** Thick collar with built-in speakers — constant low hum, can amplify to sonic attack
- **Gauntlets:** Covered in textured surfaces — sharp points, rough sandpaper, slimy gel pads, vibration panels
- **Boots:** Knee-high, mirror-like reflective material
- **Movement:** Sudden, unpredictable, jerky — never smooth

---

## MASK MASTER (Villain — The Concealer)

- **Face:** Covered by smooth porcelain-white theatrical mask — frozen exaggerated smile — dark shadowy voids where eyes should be, swirling purple-black smoke
- **Coat:** Long flowing coat made of **hundreds of overlapping masks** — comedy/tragedy masks, blank faces, happy faces, "normal" faces — layered like scales. Deep smoky charcoal gray with dark purple in shadows. Masks shift expressions when you're not looking directly
- **Under coat:** Fitted dark purple suit with near-invisible black pinstripes
- **Gloves:** Black silk — fingertips produce masks that attach to people's faces (forcing them to hide true emotions)
- **Belt:** Chain of small connected masks — each a different expression
- **Movement:** Silent, almost gliding — leaves no footprints
- **Voice:** Layered — multiple tones simultaneously — none his true voice

---

## THE ISOLATOR (Villain — The Disconnector)

- **Build:** Towering, gaunt — well over 6 feet, unnaturally thin elongated frame — looks stretched like a shadow at sunset
- **Skin:** Deep cold **charcoal gray** — matte, light-absorbing — drains color and warmth
- **Face:** Long and hollow — deeply sunken cheeks, sharp pointed chin
- **Eyes:** Narrow, deep empty **black** — no whites, no pupils — like looking into two voids
- **Head:** Bald and smooth — faint dark vein-like lines across scalp/neck pulsing with **dim cold blue light** (severed connections)
- **Coat:** Floor-length, solidified-darkness material — matte black, no texture, no reflection. Merges with shadows. High stiff collar rising past jaw
- **Hands:** Long, bony, claw-like with elongated fingers — projects dark translucent **isolation bubble force fields**
- **Torso:** Dark shadowy tendrils wrapped like chains — slowly writhe and reach outward — extend to create walls between people
- **Temperature effect:** Wherever he walks — temperature drops, sounds muffle, colors fade to gray
- **Boots:** Tall, featureless black — leaves no footprints
