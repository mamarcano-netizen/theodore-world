📖 Super Theo & Flutter — Character Gallery

Every hero and villain — illustrated & downloadable!
🦸 The Heroes 💙

Theodore

The Boy Who Sees the World Differently

A 3-year-old boy from New York with big curious eyes and the warmest smile. Theodore is non-verbal and on the autism spectrum. He notices patterns in clouds, hears music in rain, and sees beauty in spinning wheels. He is extraordinary — just as he is.

👀 Sees What Others Miss🎵 Hears the World's Music💛 Heart Full of Wonder🌀 Loves Spinning Things

Super Theo

The Spectrum Guardian

Born when the Spectrum Orb chose a boy who sees the world differently. Puzzle-piece suit, rainbow cape, mismatched boots — different is extraordinary!

🎯 Hyperfocus Beam🛡️ Empathy Shield🔍 Detail Vision💎 Truth Pulse

Flutter

The Spectrum Butterfly — Theo's One & Only Sidekick

Born from the same Spectrum Orb, Flutter's wings shimmer with every color of the rainbow. She guides Theo, calms him, and leaves sparkles wherever she flies!

🌊 Calm Aura✨ Sparkle Trail🧭 Spectrum Sense💫 Joy Boost

Daddy Anthony

Theodore's Father — Army Veteran & Advocate

A brave, loving father who never once saw his son as broken. "You are not less. You are more." He built Theodore's World so no parent ever feels alone.

💙 Unconditional Love🛡️ Fierce Protector📢 Autism Advocate
🦹 The Villains (The Conformists) ⚡

Dr. Norm

The Conformist — Leader of the Villains

Believes "different is dangerous." Wants every child the same with his Normality Serum and Cannon. Deep down, just scared of being different himself.

🧪 Normality Serum💥 Normality Cannon🔬 Conformity Science

Sensory Siren

Queen of Overwhelm

Blasts sounds and flashing lights so painful that children crumble. Represents the real sensory challenges autistic kids face every day.

📢 Sonic Screech💥 Sensory Overload⚡ Flash Blitz

Mask Master

The Identity Thief

Places blank white masks on children so they forget who they really are. Represents the pressure kids feel to hide their true selves.

🎭 Blank Masks🌫️ Identity Erase👤 Shadow Creep

The Isolator

Architect of Loneliness

A dark, ethereal being who builds invisible walls between people. Represents the loneliness and isolation autistic children often experience.

🕳️ Invisible Walls💔 Connection Break🌑 Void of Silence
